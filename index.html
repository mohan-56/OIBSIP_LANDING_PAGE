Popular Sports

Football

Football, also known as soccer in some regions, is a popular team sport played worldwide. It involves two teams of 11 players each, aiming to score goals by kicking a ball into the opposing team's net.

Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport played with a bat and ball, primarily in countries such as England, Australia, India, and many others. It is a team sport involving two teams of eleven players each.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a popular team sport played worldwide, known for its fast-paced and dynamic nature. It involves two teams of six players each, aiming to score points by hitting a ball over a net and into the opposing team's court.
